Bisquets

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 teaspoons salt
  • 90 greams of butter, 1 bar
  • 4 grams dry yeast
  • 4 cups flour
  • 1/4 cups cow's milk
  • 1 egg, to varnish
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder

Instructions

  1. We make a volcano with the flour and spread the other ingredients starting with solids and liquids and make a dough with our hands.
  2. We let the dough ferment for 15 minutes.
  3. Spread the dough with the rolling pin until it is 1 ½ centimeters thick and fold it in half.
  4. With a biscuit cutter, cut the dough. Reuse the excess, re-knead and form more bísquets.
  5. Varnish with egg and let stand for 20 more minutes.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes at approximately 180 ° C or until golden brown.
